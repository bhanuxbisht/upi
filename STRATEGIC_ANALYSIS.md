## 1. Competitive Reality Check

The Indian fintech market is crowded, but PayWise has a unique, unoccupied position.

| Competitor | What They Do | What They DON'T Do |
|-----------|-------------|-------------------|
| **CRED** | Credit card bill payments + rewards | Doesn't compare UPI apps against each other |
| **CashKaro** | Coupon aggregator + cashback | Doesn't compare payment methods for a specific transaction |
| **Kiwi** | RuPay credit card via UPI | Only promotes their own card |
| **Super.money** | UPI cashback on their app | Only their own offers, not cross-platform |
| **PhonePe/GPay/Paytm** | Their own cashback offers | Won't tell you a competitor has a better deal |

**The Unfair Advantage:** PayWise is the **only neutral, cross-platform** recommendation engine. PhonePe will never tell users "use GPay for this transaction." That is your moat.
