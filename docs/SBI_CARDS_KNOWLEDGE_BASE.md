## UNIVERSAL SBI EXCLUSIONS (apply to most cards unless stated otherwise)
- Fuel purchases at non-co-brand pumps (earn 0 RP on most cards)
- Wallet loads (Paytm, PhonePe, etc.) — excluded on most cards
- Property / Rent payments — excluded on cashback cards
- EMI / Flexipay transactions — no cashback
- Government transactions — excluded on co-brand airline cards
